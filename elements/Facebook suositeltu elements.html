🚨 BREAKING NEWS 🚨

TKO has finalised an agreement with Perth to bring several events to the city, including a WWE premium live event.

WWE previously held the Elimination Chamber in Perth, Western Australia, in February 2024 at Optus Stadium. The organization has been actively pursuing international events but has not yet returned to Australia.

According to The West Australian Newspaper, Perth is expected to host a series of TKO events soon, featuring a tripleheader of WWE Raw, Smackdown, and an upcoming premium live event in 2025 This deal, which also includes two UFC events, will see the RAC Arena host these shows over the next 18 months, with WWE's events scheduled for later in 2025. TKO reportedly reached an agreement with the Western Australian government some time ago.
